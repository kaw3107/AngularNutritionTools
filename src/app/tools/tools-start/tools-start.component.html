Calorie Calculator Nutrition App's

The Calorie Calculator is a good way to get started with you're weight loss journey. The app allows you to customise you're macronutrients so that you are able to plan ahead with your meals. The tool is ideal for calorie tracking and meal planning on calorie restricted diets.

IIFYM Calculator Nutrition App's

IIFYM follows the moto of 'If It Fits Your Macros Eat It'. The IIFYM Calculator will give you a calculated result of you're macronutrients customised to you're profile. IIFYM is great for Bulking, Maintaining and Weight Loss and the app will give you a calculated result for you're goals.

BMR Calculator Nutrition App's

BMR is a measurement of the number of calories needed to perform your body's most basic (basal) functions, like breathing, circulation and cell production. The BMR calculator is usual to find out the base number of calories you need to survive on day-to-day.
